Step 0: Choose the Shop

Open your food delivery app and look at the list of boba shops.

Now check today’s month and date. Add them together.

Example: If today is March 18, 3 + 18 = 21

Now look at the last digit of that number.

21 → last digit is 1

Starting from the top of the shop list, count down to that number.

That is today’s chosen shop.

If the number is 0, treat it as 10.
